We are Lisa and Tobias, We are the Founders of RetailerGuru. We got to know each other through our joint project. With RetailerGuru we want to make the world a little bit better. Come with us into an innovative future.

We make your weekly shopping easier with RetailerGuru . We save on driving distances and thus pay attention to our environment. Unnecessary searches for your favorite products and the disappointment when they are sold out, are now a thing of the past

For our Environment.

Since the environment is very important to us, we are happy to be able to do our part with our application. In this way we can also offer future generations a world worth living in.

Trust your Partners

Constant Improvement
